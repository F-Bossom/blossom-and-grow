## Description

**Blossom & Grow** is a cozy plant care tracker app built for plant lovers who want to keep their leafy friends happy and healthy! Track your houseplants, log watering and sunlight, and watch your plants' moods change based on how well you care for them.


## 🚀 Getting Started

- **Deployed App:** [Blossom & Grow](https://blossom-and-grow-58935134392e.herokuapp.com/)
- **Planning Materials:** [Trello Board](https://trello.com/b/2ueLsLF3/blossom-grow)


## ✨ Features

- 🌱 Add and manage your plant collection with nicknames, varieties, and notes
- 💧 Log watering and sunlight care entries with custom dates
- 😊 Dynamic mood system — plants react to how well they're cared for!
- 🔒 Secure authentication — your garden is private to you
- 🌈 Seven plant families, each with unique care thresholds

### Mood States
Each plant can be in one of these moods based on their care history:
| Mood | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🌈 Thriving | Perfect water and sunlight |
| 😊 Happy | Well cared for |
| 💧 Thirsty | Needs water soon |
| 💧💧 Very Thirsty | Needs water urgently |
| ☀️ Needs Sun | Could use more light |
| 🌑 Needs More Sun | Very sun deprived |
| 💦 Overwatered | Too much water |
| 🔥 Too Much Sun | Too much direct light |
| 😢 Neglected | Needs attention on all fronts |
